compute_dynamic_payout() — trigger payout formula
payout = base_earning
× (rain_intensity / 10) ← intensity_factor
× (duration / 60) ← duration_factor
× plan_multiplier ← 0.4 / 0.6 / 0.8
premium = base + Σ adjustments
+ flood_risk × 18
+ aqi_index × 6
+ disruption_freq × 2.5
– clad_score_discount
+ new_user_surcharge
– streak_discount
+ monsoon_surcharge (if Jun–Sep)
final = max(base, computed)
